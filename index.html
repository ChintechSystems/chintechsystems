About Chintech Systems

Gujarat's Most Trusted IT Infrastructure Partner Since 1995

Founded by Mr. Kashyap Doshi, Chintech Systems began with a clear mission: make enterprise-grade IT accessible to businesses across Gujarat. Over three decades, we have grown into a 55+ professional organisation serving corporations, universities, hospitals, and government bodies across India — and internationally.

We don't just supply hardware. We design, deploy, and manage complete IT ecosystems — from the server room to every network endpoint — and stay on as your long-term technology partner through our FMS and AMC programmes.
